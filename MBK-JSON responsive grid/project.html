Lysee Stop Motion

This project created under the direction of Geoffry Dunne at JWALK, was more of an experiment to see what the agency could create with previous photos that lysee had from their most recent photoshoot. The end result being a stop motion highlighting some of the new spring/summer looks they were getting ready to launch. This work was launched as both a 10 and 15 second commercial spot on youtube.
