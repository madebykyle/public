elcome to MadeByKyle, cliché way to start I know, but at this point what wouldn’t be, this is an introductory paragraph after all and not just to my site but to myself as well, so why not start with a very cordial greeting. I am a interaction/motion designer currently residing in NYC and a graduate of Parsons: The New School, and this is my current version of how I want to exposé my work to the world.

There is a little bit of everything, spread out all over the site, from random JavaScript projects on one of the pages of the slider below, to cinemagraph spreads and other barely noticeable little details that I tend to pride myself in. I hope you enjoy my work and click through to find a few of the hidden gems.
